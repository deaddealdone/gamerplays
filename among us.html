Among Us

"Among Us" is an online multiplayer game developed and published by the American game studio InnerSloth. It was initially released for iOS and Android devices in June 2018 and later made available for Windows and macOS in November 2018. The game gained immense popularity in 2020, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic. The gameplay of "Among Us" revolves around a group of players who are crew members aboard a spaceship or a space station. The crew members have various tasks to complete around the map to ensure the ship's smooth operation. However, among the crew members, there are one to three impostors whose goal is to sabotage the ship and kill off the crew without getting caught. The game combines elements of social deduction and teamwork. The crew members must work together to identify the impostors through discussions and voting. The impostors, on the other hand, must deceive the crew and eliminate them one by one. The game ends when either all the tasks are completed, in which case the crew members win, or when the impostors outnumber the crew members, resulting in an impostor victory. "Among Us" became highly popular due to its simple gameplay mechanics and the ability to play with friends or strangers online. It gained significant attention on platforms like Twitch and YouTube, with many content creators streaming their gameplay sessions. The game's popularity led to its availability on various gaming platforms and a large player base worldwide. It's important to note that my knowledge is based on information available up until September 2021, so there may have been updates or changes to "Among Us" since then.
