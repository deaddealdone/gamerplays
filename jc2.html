Just cause 2

"Just Cause 2" is an action-adventure video game developed by Avalanche Studios and published by Square Enix. It was released in 2010 and is the second installment in the "Just Cause" series. The game is set on the fictional island of Panau, a vast open world with diverse environments including jungles, deserts, mountains, and cities. In "Just Cause 2," players assume the role of Rico Rodriguez, a skilled operative for a fictional US government agency called the Agency. Rico is tasked with overthrowing the island's oppressive dictator, Pandak "Baby" Panay, and assisting the local resistance movement known as the Reapers. The game offers players a wide range of tools and abilities to cause chaos and complete missions. Rico has a grappling hook that allows him to navigate the environment quickly and perform stunts. He can also use a parachute to glide through the air and perform mid-air stunts or base jumps. "Just Cause 2" emphasizes open-world exploration and offers a vast map for players to discover. The game features a wide array of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, boats, helicopters, and even fighter jets. These vehicles can be used for transportation or as weapons of destruction. The main storyline of the game follows Rico as he completes missions for the Reapers and other factions, causing chaos and destabilizing Panau's government. However, players are also encouraged to explore the open world freely, engage in side activities, and wreak havoc using the game's physics-based mechanics and explosive action. Overall, "Just Cause 2" is known for its expansive open world, high-octane action, and freedom of gameplay, allowing players to create their own chaotic and explosive adventures.



Requirements
Dual-core CPU with SSE3 (Athlon 64 X2 4200 / Pentium D 3GHz)
2 GB of RAM
DX10 compatible graphics card with 256 MB of memory (After changing graphic setting, you can play it without graphic card as well).
